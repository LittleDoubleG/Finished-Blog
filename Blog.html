Amazon Prime's Latest Materpiece... Invincible

Invincible is Great!

My dad is the one who introduced me to the show. I watched the first episode and was instantly hooked. The first episode makes the viewers want to come back for more, leaving the episode on a shocking cliffhanger. This show is for a more mature audience and honestly, I think that's what makes it so good.

These are the good things that this show managed to accomplish:

1. It gave a reason for the viewers to keep watching.

2. It shows a more realistic view on superheroes.

3. It also shows the human side of the superheroes.
